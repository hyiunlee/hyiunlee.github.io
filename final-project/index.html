ANIMAL TESTING

Main About Changes Products Sources

Animal testing refers to any scientific experiments and procedures carried out on living animals for the purposes of basic biology and disease research, evaluating the efficacy of new medicinal products, and testing for the human health and safety of consumer and industrial products such as cosmetics, food additives, and industrial chemicals. It is used because they are biologically comparable to humans and hence make excellent models.
