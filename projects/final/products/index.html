How to know if a product is cruelty-free?


Cruelty-free cosmetics are free from animal testing at all phases of product development. However, it is important to note that these cosmetics differ from vegan cosmetics as they may still contain animal-derived ingredients; Cruelty-free merely ensures that the end product and its ingredients are not tested on animals.
