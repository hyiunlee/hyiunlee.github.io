The Scandal That Shook the K-pop Industry

May 5, 2019

In February 2019, one of the biggest scandals arose to flip the image of the K-pop industry upside down. A scandal that originally started off with one person has uncovered the involvement of at least six more celebrities (with more to be revealed). It all began two months ago with one of K-pop’s biggest stars, a member of Big Bang, Seungri. CCTV footage of an assault was found at a club he owned called “Burning Sun.” Since then, allegations have been held against a group of people for sexual assault and bribing of the police community.
